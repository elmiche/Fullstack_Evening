Books

The martian is a sci-fi novel, which was later adapted into a movie starring some white guy. It's cool because the astronaut is also a botanist and he ends up stranded on mars for years, surviving all on his own.

The martian chronicles is a collection of short-stories by Author Ray Bradbury. He makes many predictions about the future of space travel, technology and society that we can see today.

Movies

Interstellar is a sci-fi movie about deep-space exploration, that covers topics like interdimentional time-travel and physics.

Jurassic Park a sci-fi movie (based on a book) about bringing dinosaurs back to life using genetics and molecular biology techniques.
